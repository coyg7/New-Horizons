Graduate Management Admission Test (GMAT) is a Test taken to get admission to MBA colleges worldwide in various Universities and Business Schools.

GMAT is used to promote fairness, objectivity and validity in selection, and provides supplementary information to that contained in your application form for your MBA admission.

It DOES NOT test knowledge in specialized academic subjects, but provides an indication of the ability for clear and systematic thought.

Why the GMAT® Exam?
  • Accepted by more than 6000 business and management programs worldwide
  • The GMAT exam measures the skills you have and the skills schools need
  • The GMAT exam is relied on more by graduate business schools worldwide
  • GMAT based exam taken by all leading Universities and Colleges of Nepal for MBA Admission
